## Topics Covered

The seminar provided comprehensive knowledge on building robust IT infrastructure using open-source virtualization and professional routing solutions. Proxmox VE is a powerful enterprise virtualization platform that combines KVM hypervisor and LXC containers, offering a complete solution for managing virtual machines and containers through a web-based interface.

MikroTik RouterOS integration was covered extensively, demonstrating how to configure professional-grade routing, firewall rules, and network management. The combination of Proxmox VE for virtualization and MikroTik for network routing creates a cost-effective yet powerful infrastructure solution suitable for businesses of various sizes.

Key areas included virtual machine deployment and management, container orchestration, network configuration and segmentation, router setup and optimization, and integration strategies between virtualization platforms and network infrastructure.

## Why It Matters

Understanding both virtualization and network routing is essential for modern infrastructure management. Proxmox VE offers enterprise-level virtualization capabilities without licensing costs, making it an excellent choice for organizations looking to optimize their infrastructure budget. MikroTik routers are widely used in Indonesia and globally for their powerful features at competitive prices.

This seminar provided practical knowledge for designing and implementing scalable, secure, and efficient IT infrastructure. The skills gained are directly applicable to building lab environments, production systems, and cloud-like infrastructure on-premise.
